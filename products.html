Product Principles

What Every Gliders Tech Product Guarantees

These are not aspirations — they are confirmed by the design and legal documentation of every application we publish.

📡

Offline by Design

Core product functionality never requires an internet connection. Internet is used only when a specific optional feature — such as cloud backup or audio download — explicitly needs it.

🔐

Data Stays on Your Device

Application data is stored locally. We do not transmit, sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. This applies to every product without exception.

📋

Transparent Documentation

Every product has its own Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, and Support page. Legal documents are written in plain language — no ambiguity about how your data is handled.

🔄

Actively Maintained

Products receive updates based on user feedback, bug reports, and Android platform changes. We do not publish and abandon — each product is a long-term commitment.

🛡️

Minimum Permissions

Each product requests only the permissions genuinely required by its features — camera for scanning, storage for saving files, notifications for reminders. Nothing unnecessary.

💬

Real Support Included

Every product ships with a dedicated support channel. We aim to respond to every inquiry within 2–5 business days at no additional cost to the user.
