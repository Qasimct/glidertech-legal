Quran Majeed Offline Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Thank you for using Quran Majeed Offline, developed by Gliders Tech. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information and protects your privacy.

Offline Quran Reading

The Holy Quran text and the core reading experience are available offline. You can read the Quran without creating an account or providing personal information.

Audio Streaming & Downloads

If you choose to stream or download Quran recitations, the application uses an Internet connection only for that purpose. Downloaded audio files are stored locally on your device for offline listening.

Information Stored Locally

To improve your experience, the application may locally store:

  • Bookmarks
  • Last Read Position
  • Reading Preferences
  • Theme Settings
  • Downloaded Audio Information

This information remains on your device and is not uploaded to Gliders Tech servers.

Internet Usage

Internet access is required only for optional features such as audio streaming and audio downloads. Reading the Quran itself works completely offline.

Data Sharing

Gliders Tech does not sell, rent, or intentionally share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards to help protect locally stored application data. However, no electronic storage system can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Quran Majeed Offline is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

  • Access your locally stored information.
  • Delete application data.
  • Remove downloaded audio files.
  • Uninstall the application at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
