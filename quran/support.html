Support Center

Welcome to the official support page for Quran Majeed Offline. If you experience any issues or have suggestions, we are happy to assist you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I read the Quran without an Internet connection?

Yes. The Holy Quran text is available completely offline.

Why is Internet required?

Internet access is used only for optional features such as audio streaming and downloading Quran recitations.

Where are my bookmarks stored?

Bookmarks, last read position, and application settings are stored locally on your device.

Can I listen offline?

Yes. After downloading audio recitations, you can listen without an Internet connection.

Troubleshooting

  • Update the application to the latest version.
  • Ensure your Internet connection is available for audio downloads.
  • Restart the application if audio playback stops unexpectedly.
  • Verify that sufficient device storage is available before downloading audio.
  • Restart your device if unexpected issues occur.

Bug Reports

To help us resolve issues quickly, please include:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • Application version
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots (if available)
