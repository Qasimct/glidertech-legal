Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. The application is designed using an offline-first architecture. Internet is only required for optional features such as Google Drive Backup and Google Sign-In.

Where is my data stored?

Your business data is stored locally on your own device unless you choose to create a backup in your personal Google Drive.

Can I restore my data on another phone?

Yes. You can restore your database using the Backup & Restore feature.

Can I change my gym logo later?

Yes. The Business Profile section allows you to update your gym name, logo, contact information, and address at any time.

Does the app support QR attendance?

Yes. QR Code scanning is available for attendance management.

Report a Bug

When reporting an issue, please include:
