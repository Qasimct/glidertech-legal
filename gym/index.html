The information may include: Gym Information Gym Logo Owner Information Member Details Trainer Details Membership Plans Attendance Records Payment History Receipts Reports Backup Files How We Use Information Manage gym members Generate payment receipts Track attendance Create reports Generate PDF invoices Backup your database Restore your backup Camera Permission The Camera permission is used only for scanning QR Codes during attendance management. Notifications Notifications are used to remind gym owners about: Membership renewals Expired memberships Pending payments Notification data never leaves your device. Google Drive Backup If you choose to connect your Google account, the application can back up your database to your personal Google Drive. We do not have access to your Google Drive files. The files remain under your own Google account. Internet Usage Internet access is used only for optional features such as Google Sign-In and Google Drive Backup. No member information is transmitted to Glider Tech servers. PDF Generation The application generates PDF receipts and reports locally on your device. Generated documents are not uploaded to our servers. Data Storage All operational data is stored locally using an offline-first architecture. Your information remains under your control. Data Sharing We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Data Security We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Children's Privacy Our application is intended for gym owners and business operators. It is not directed toward children.
