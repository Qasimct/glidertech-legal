Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Thank you for using Gym Management System developed by Glider Tech. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

Our application primarily stores information locally on your device. The information may include:

  • Gym Information
  • Gym Logo
  • Owner Information
  • Member Details
  • Trainer Details
  • Membership Plans
  • Attendance Records
  • Payment History
  • Receipts
  • Reports
  • Backup Files

How We Use Information

  • Manage gym members
  • Generate payment receipts
  • Track attendance
  • Create reports
  • Generate PDF invoices
  • Backup your database
  • Restore your backup

Camera Permission

The Camera permission is used only for scanning QR Codes during attendance management. No camera data is uploaded to our servers.

Photos & Images

The application allows you to choose images from your device for:

  • Gym Logo
  • Member Photos
  • Trainer Photos

Selected images remain on your device unless you choose to back them up.

Notifications

Notifications are used to remind gym owners about:

  • Membership renewals
  • Expired memberships
  • Pending payments

Notification data never leaves your device.

Google Drive Backup

If you choose to connect your Google account, the application can back up your database to your personal Google Drive.

We do not have access to your Google Drive files. The files remain under your own Google account.

Internet Usage

Internet access is used only for optional features such as Google Sign-In and Google Drive Backup. No member information is transmitted to Glider Tech servers.

PDF Generation

The application generates PDF receipts and reports locally on your device. Generated documents are not uploaded to our servers.

Data Storage

All operational data is stored locally using an offline-first architecture. Your information remains under your control.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our application is intended for gym owners and business operators. It is not directed toward children.

Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Create backups
  • Restore backups
  • Remove Google account access

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
