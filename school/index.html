School Book Registry Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how School Book Registry, developed by Gliders Tech, handles your information. We are committed to transparency and protecting the privacy of everyone who uses this application.

Introduction

School Book Registry is a record-keeping application built for teachers and school staff to manage the distribution of books to students. We take your privacy seriously and have designed this application to collect only the minimum data necessary to perform its core function.

Information We Collect

This application only processes data that you manually enter. This may include:

  • School name and district
  • Student name and father’s name
  • Student CNIC or B-Form number
  • Book distribution records and class details

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following at any time:

  • Location or GPS data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Phone contacts or call logs
  • Payment or credit card information
  • Advertising IDs or tracking identifiers

How Your Data Is Stored

All data entered in this application is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to our servers. If you choose to use the optional Google Drive backup feature, your data is saved directly to your own Google account — we do not have access to your Google Drive data.

Third-Party Services

This application optionally integrates with Google Drive for backup purposes only. Google’s own privacy policy governs any data stored there. You can review it at: href="https://policies.google.com/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> policies.google.com/privacy

Children’s Privacy

School Book Registry is intended exclusively for use by teachers and school administrators. This application is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. Student records entered by school staff are managed solely by the authorized adult user of the application.

Data Security

Your data remains on your device and is not shared, sold, or transferred to any third party under any circumstances. We take reasonable precautions to ensure the data you enter within this application remains secure.

Your Rights

  • You may delete all data stored by this application at any time by uninstalling the application from your device.
  • This action permanently removes all locally stored records with no data retained by us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised Last Updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.
