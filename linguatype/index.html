Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Thank you for using LinguaType Pro, developed by Gliders Tech. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the application may use, how it is processed, and how your data is protected.

Keyboard Functionality

LinguaType Pro is a software keyboard designed to help users enter text on their devices. The application processes your typing only to provide keyboard functionality such as text input, predictions, suggestions, and personalization.

Typed Text

Text entered through LinguaType Pro is processed on your device to provide keyboard features. We do not intentionally collect, store, or transmit your personal typed content to Gliders Tech servers.

Application Settings

The application may locally store settings such as:

  • Selected language
  • Keyboard theme
  • Keyboard preferences
  • Typing settings
  • User customization

These settings remain stored on your own device.

Internet Usage

Certain optional features may require an Internet connection. Core keyboard functionality remains available according to the application's supported features.

Data Sharing

Gliders Tech does not sell, rent, or intentionally share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

Reasonable technical safeguards are implemented to help protect application data. However, no electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

LinguaType Pro is intended for general users. The application is not specifically directed toward children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

  • Modify application settings.
  • Clear application data.
  • Uninstall the application at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
