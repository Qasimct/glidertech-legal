Support Center

Welcome to the official support page for LinguaType Pro. If you have questions, encounter technical issues, or would like to suggest improvements, our support team is here to help.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable LinguaType Pro?

Open your device's keyboard settings, enable LinguaType Pro, and then select it as your default keyboard.

Does LinguaType Pro work offline?

Yes. Core keyboard functionality is designed to work offline. Some optional features may require an Internet connection.

Does LinguaType Pro collect my typed text?

No. LinguaType Pro is designed to process typing locally to provide keyboard functionality. We do not intentionally collect or store your personal typed content.

How can I change the keyboard theme?

Open the application's settings and select your preferred theme from the available customization options.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart your device if the keyboard does not appear.
  • Ensure LinguaType Pro is enabled in system settings.
  • Update the application to the latest version.
  • Clear the application cache if you experience unexpected behavior.
  • Restart the application after changing keyboard settings.

Bug Reports

To help us investigate an issue, please include:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • LinguaType Pro version
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots (if available)

Feature Requests
