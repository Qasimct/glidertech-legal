Frequently Asked Questions

Does DocMaster Pro work offline?

Yes. Most PDF processing features work completely offline.

Are my documents uploaded to Glider Tech?

No. Your files remain under your control unless you choose to use optional cloud services.

Why does the app request Camera permission?

Only for document scanning.

Why is Storage permission required?

To open, save, export, and manage your PDF files.

How do Premium features work?

Premium purchases are securely handled through Google Play Billing.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart the application.
  • Update to the latest version.
  • Verify storage permissions.
  • Check your internet connection for cloud features.
  • Restart your device if necessary.

Report a Bug
