DocMaster Pro Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to DocMaster Pro, developed by Gliders Tech. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information our application may access, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Application Overview

DocMaster Pro is an advanced offline-first PDF reader, document scanner, OCR toolkit, PDF creator, PDF editor, and document management application. Most document processing occurs locally on your device.

Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, the application may access:

  • PDF Documents
  • Office Documents
  • Images
  • Scanned Documents
  • OCR Text
  • Application Preferences
  • Cloud Backup Metadata
  • Crash Reports
  • Anonymous Analytics

How We Use Information

  • Open PDF files
  • Create PDF documents
  • Merge PDF files
  • Split PDF files
  • Compress PDF files
  • Convert Images to PDF
  • Extract text using OCR
  • Secure documents
  • Synchronize optional cloud backups
  • Improve application stability

Camera Permission

Camera access is used only when you choose to scan documents. Captured images remain under your control unless you intentionally upload or share them.

Storage Permission

Storage access is required to:

  • Open existing documents
  • Save generated PDFs
  • Import images
  • Export files
  • Restore backups

DocMaster Pro never modifies your personal files without your action.

OCR Processing

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is used to extract text from scanned documents and images. OCR results are generated only for the files you select.

Cloud Services

If you choose cloud features, your files may be synchronized with your own cloud account. Glider Tech does not claim ownership of your documents.

Firebase Services

DocMaster Pro may use Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Remote Config

These services help improve reliability and user experience.

Advertisements

The application may display Google AdMob advertisements. Google may collect limited anonymous advertising information according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Premium Features

Premium subscriptions and purchases are securely processed through Google Play Billing. Payment information is never stored by Glider Tech.

Biometric Security

If enabled, fingerprint or biometric authentication is used only to unlock the application on your device. Biometric information never leaves your device.

Notifications

Notifications may remind you about document tasks, backups, or important application events.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information. However, no electronic storage method can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

DocMaster Pro is not intended for children under the applicable minimum age.

Your Rights

  • Access your files
  • Delete your data
  • Remove cloud connections
  • Export your documents
  • Uninstall the application at any time

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
