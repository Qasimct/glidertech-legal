Before You Write

Frequently Asked Questions

These answers cover the most common questions we receive. If yours is not here, send us a message.

How long does it take to receive a reply?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 2–5 business days. Response times may vary depending on the volume of requests we receive.

Which email address should I use to contact you?

Send all enquiries to href="mailto:official.gliderstech@gmail.com" style="color:var(--primary); font-weight:600;"> official.gliderstech@gmail.com . This is the only official contact address for Gliders Tech.

My data is not saving between app sessions. What should I do?

All Gliders Tech applications store data locally on your device. If data is missing, check that the app has not been cleared from storage settings. See the relevant app support page for troubleshooting steps.

Can I request a refund for a premium purchase?

Premium purchases are processed through Google Play. Refund requests are governed by Google Play's refund policy and must be submitted through your Google Play account.

Do you accept feature requests?

Yes. We welcome suggestions for all Gliders Tech applications. Popular or frequently requested features may be considered for future updates. Select "Feature Request" as the subject when writing to us.

I have a privacy question about one of your applications. Who do I contact?

Send privacy-related questions to href="mailto:official.gliderstech@gmail.com" style="color:var(--primary); font-weight:600;"> official.gliderstech@gmail.com with "Privacy Enquiry" as the subject. You can also review the Privacy Policy for each application directly on its dedicated page.
