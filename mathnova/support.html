Support Center

Welcome to the official support page for MathNova. If you experience any issues or have questions about the application, we are here to help.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MathNova free?

MathNova may offer free and premium features depending on the version you are using.

Does MathNova require an Internet connection?

Most features are designed to work offline. Some future features may require Internet access.

Does MathNova collect personal information?

No. MathNova is designed to operate primarily on your device and does not require personal information for its core features.

How can I report a problem?

Please send us a detailed description of the issue, including your device model, Android version, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Before Contacting Support

  • Update MathNova to the latest version.
  • Restart your device.
  • Check available device storage.
  • Try reproducing the issue.
  • Include screenshots if possible.

Feature Requests

We welcome suggestions for new mathematical tools, calculators, learning features, and improvements. Your feedback helps us improve MathNova.

Bug Reports

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • MathNova version
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots (if available)

Response Time

We aim to respond to support requests within 2–5 business days. Actual response times may vary depending on request volume.
