MathNova Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Thank you for using MathNova, developed by Gliders Tech. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be stored, how it is used, and how your privacy is protected.

Information We Collect

MathNova is designed as an offline-first educational application. The application does not require users to create an account or provide personal information to use its core features. Depending on the features you use, the application may locally store:

  • Calculator history
  • Favorite formulas
  • Learning preferences
  • Theme settings
  • Application settings
  • Recent activity

How We Use Information

  • Provide mathematical tools and calculators.
  • Remember your application preferences.
  • Improve your learning experience.
  • Restore previously saved settings.

Local Storage

Application preferences and settings are stored locally on your own device. Your information remains under your control unless you choose to remove the application or clear its stored data.

Internet Usage

The core functionality of MathNova works without requiring continuous Internet access. If future online features are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Sharing

Gliders Tech does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are implemented to help protect locally stored application data. However, no electronic storage method can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

MathNova is an educational application intended for students, teachers, and general users. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

  • Access your locally stored data.
  • Clear application data.
  • Uninstall the application at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect application improvements or legal requirements. The latest version will always be published on this page.
