Privacy Policy

How the Gliders Tech website handles information and how our applications protect your privacy.

Overview

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gliders Tech handles information in connection with this website (hosted on GitHub Pages) and provides a summary of our approach to privacy across all Gliders Tech Android applications.

Each Gliders Tech Android application has its own dedicated Privacy Policy. This page covers the website itself and our company-wide privacy principles.

This Website

The Gliders Tech website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. It does not use cookies, does not run server-side scripts, and does not collect personal information from visitors through the website itself.

GitHub, as the hosting provider, may collect standard server access logs including IP addresses and browser information in accordance with href="https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> GitHub's Privacy Statement . Gliders Tech does not have access to or control over these server logs.

Contact Form

The contact form on this website uses your device's email client to send messages directly to official.gliderstech@gmail.com . No form data is transmitted to, processed by, or stored on any Gliders Tech server. The information you choose to include in your email is handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Email Communications

When you contact Gliders Tech by email, we receive the information you choose to provide — such as your name, email address, and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not use contact information for marketing purposes and do not share it with third parties.

Our Android Applications

Gliders Tech develops offline-first Android applications. Each application follows these core privacy principles:

  • Application data is stored locally on the user's device by default.
  • Personal information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.
  • Internet access is used only when a specific feature explicitly requires it, such as cloud backup or audio downloads.
  • Only the permissions genuinely required by each feature are requested.

For the full Privacy Policy of each application, please visit the relevant page below:

  • MathNova — Privacy Policy
  • LinguaType Pro — Privacy Policy
  • Gym Management System — Privacy Policy
  • Quran Majeed Offline — Privacy Policy
  • DocMaster Pro — Privacy Policy

Third-Party Services

Some Gliders Tech Android applications use third-party services to support optional features. Where applicable, these include:

  • Google Drive API — used only when the user chooses to enable cloud backup. Files are stored in the user's own Google account.
  • Google Sign-In — used only for optional cloud features in applicable applications.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — used in DocMaster Pro to improve reliability and user experience.
  • Google AdMob — used in the free version of DocMaster Pro. Google may collect limited anonymous advertising information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — used for premium purchases in DocMaster Pro. Payment information is never stored by Gliders Tech.

Each of these services is governed by its own terms and privacy policies. Gliders Tech is not responsible for the data practices of third-party services.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed toward children. Gliders Tech does not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website. Individual application policies contain specific guidance on children's privacy for each app.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are in place to protect any information handled by Gliders Tech. However, no electronic transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Rights

  • Request information about any personal data we hold as a result of direct email communication with us.
  • Request deletion of any such data by contacting us directly.
  • Uninstall any Gliders Tech application at any time to remove locally stored app data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to this website or our applications. The latest version will always be published on this page. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
